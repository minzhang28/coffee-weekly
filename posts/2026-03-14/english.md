# This Week's Coffee Picks

This week’s theme is Peak Season. We’ve selected a few coffees that are at their absolute prime right now, from an intensely fermented experimental Colombian natural to a classic, clean Ethiopian. We've got some great picks for espresso too.

---

## Pour Over Recommendations

### Pick 1: [Colombia - Finca Bella Alejandria Wilder Lazo | Geisha Natural 300 hour](https://roguewavecoffee.ca/products/colombia-finca-bella-alejandria-wilder-lazo-geisha-natural-300-hour)

![Colombia - Finca Bella Alejandria Wilder Lazo | Geisha Natural 300 hour](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0042/0773/8992/files/ColombiaFincaBellaAlejandriaWilderLazoGeisha300hour200gBAG.jpg?v=1773246641)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Rogue Wave
- Origin: Colombia Huila
- Variety: Striped Bourbon, Tabi, Pink Bourbon
- Process: Experimental Natural Fermentation
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $36 / 250g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Fresh arrival from Colombia's peak harvest season. The flavors are extremely active right now.

② [Rarity/Value]
- A micro-lot from renowned producer Wilder Lazo using a highly experimental process. High complexity and definitely worth exploring.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: A mix of Bourbon varieties provides a base of complex sweetness and florals.

**Tastes Like**: Heavy fermentation notes, blueberry jam, tropical fruit liqueur. Can get funky if brewed wrong.

#### Brew Guide (V60/Pour Over)
- Ratio: 1:16
- Temp: 92°C (lower temp for this high-fermentation bean prevents over-extraction of funky notes)
- Grind: Medium-fine

## Espresso Recommendations

![DAK Jazz Fruits *Natural Espresso*](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0023/8593/6496/files/ScreenShot2024-06-21at10.58.19AM.png?v=1718992724)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: DAK (via Revolver)
- Origin: Ethiopia Daye Bensa
- Variety: Local Landraces
- Process: Natural
- Roast: Medium
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $28.50 / 250g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- A freshly roasted seasonal from Ethiopia that is currently very expressive.

② [Rarity/Value]
- Pulls a very interesting shot as an SOE with great balance between juiciness and sweetness.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: The Daye Bensa region is known for producing clean and sweet natural-processed coffees.

**Tastes Like**: Strawberry, White Chocolate, Orange.

#### Brew Guide (Espresso Machine)

- Ratio: 1:2.5
- Temp: 93°C (slightly higher temp for this medium roast brings out more fruit notes)
- Grind: Fine

## How to Choose

If you're after an intense, funky, and complex pour over, grab the Wilder Lazo Colombia. For a clean, classic, and bright floral-fruity cup, the Gigesa from Ethiopia is a solid choice. For espresso, the DAK pulls a wild, juicy shot as a single origin. The Ex Animo blend is your bulletproof option for milk drinks—super chocolatey, sweet, and reliable.
