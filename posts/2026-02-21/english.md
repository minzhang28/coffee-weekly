# This Week's Coffee Picks

This week, we're highlighting peak-season coffees from Central and South America. For pour over, a classic Panamanian Geisha against a modern Colombian Pink Bourbon. On espresso, it's a wild anaerobic natural from Brazil versus a reliable, balanced blend. Peak freshness, peak flavour.

---

## Pour Over Recommendations

![Panama - Bambito Estate Geisha | Washed - 100g](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0042/0773/8992/files/PanamaBambitoEstateGeishaWashed100gBAG.jpg?v=1770056383)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Rogue Wave
- Origin: Panama Chiriqui Province, Bambito
- Variety: Geisha
- Process: Washed
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $55 / 100g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Panama is at peak harvest season. These beans are fresh, and the flavors are at their most vibrant.

② [Rarity/Value]

**Variety Character**: Geisha is known for its distinct and delicate tea-like body and floral aromatics.

**Tastes Like**: Jasmine, bergamot, white peach, honey.

- Temp: 93°C (Lower temp protects the delicate floral notes.)

## Espresso Recommendations

### Pick 1: [DAK Pretty Nuts *Anaerobic Natural Espresso*](https://revolvercoffee.ca/products/dak-pretty-nuts-bulk-1kg-anaerobic-natural-espresso)

![DAK Pretty Nuts BULK 1KG *Anaerobic Natural Espresso*](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0023/8593/6496/files/Screenshot2026-02-06at9.42.47AM.png?v=1770399772)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: DAK (via Revolver)
- Origin: Brazil Sul de Minas
- Variety: Icatu
- Process: Anaerobic Natural
- Roast: Medium
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $95 / 1kg

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Fresh crop from Brazil, processed to amplify flavour.

② [Rarity/Value]
- A modern anaerobic that pushes flavour boundaries. The 1kg bag is solid value for those who want to experiment.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Icatu is a sturdy base, and the anaerobic process adds heavy fermentation and boozy notes.

**Tastes Like**: Peanut butter, Grand Marnier, forest fruits.

#### Brew Guide (Espresso Machine)
- Dose: 18g
- Ratio: 1:2.5 (Longer ratio helps balance the intense fermentation notes and prevents sourness.)
- Temp: 92°C (Lower temp keeps the fermentation notes from becoming funky or astringent.)
- Grind: Standard espresso

## How to Choose

If you're chasing the delicate, tea-like florals of a top-tier coffee, the Panama Geisha is a must-try. For a high-value daily pour over with bright fruit notes, the Colombian Pink Bourbon is fantastic. On espresso, the DAK is a wild ride as an SOE, full of boozy, fermented character. The Ex Animo blend is the dependable choice for milk drinks—it’s forgiving, balanced, and consistently delicious.
