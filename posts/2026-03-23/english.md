# This Week's Coffee Picks

This week’s focus is on peak-season coffees from South America. We’ve selected two unique Colombian pour overs and two distinct espressos, covering the spectrum from clean and classic to wild and modern. There's a pick here for every palate.

---

## Pour Over Recommendations

### Pick 1: [COLOMBIA | Luz Helena Salazar - Finca Mazatlan](https://nemesis.coffee/products/colombia-luz-helena-salazar-finca-mazatlan)

![COLOMBIA | Luz Helena Salazar - Finca Mazatlan](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0570/9499/6149/files/Cream_Front.png?v=1771528769)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Nemesis
- Origin: Colombia Quindio
- Variety: Pink Bourbon
- Process: Washed
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $27 / 250g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Peak season for fresh crop Colombian coffee.

② [Rarity/Value]
- Pink Bourbon is a highly sought-after variety; this lot offers excellent value.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Pink Bourbon is known for delicate florals and complex sweetness.

**Tastes Like**: wildflower honey, fuji apple, berries.

#### Brew Guide (V60/Pour Over)
- Ratio: 1:16
- Temp: 96°C (high-density bean, needs the heat to properly extract acidity)
- Grind: medium-fine (like table salt)

#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Monogram (available at Revolver)
- Origin: Colombia Central Mountain Range
- Variety: SL28
- Process: Yeast Inoculated Anaerobic Natural
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $39 / 300g

② [Rarity/Value]

## Espresso Recommendations

### Pick 1: [DAK Pretty Nuts BULK 1KG *Anaerobic Natural Espresso*](https://revolvercoffee.ca/products/dak-pretty-nuts-bulk-1kg-anaerobic-natural-espresso)

![DAK Pretty Nuts BULK 1KG *Anaerobic Natural Espresso*](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0023/8593/6496/files/Screenshot2026-02-06at9.42.47AM.png?v=1770399772)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: DAK (available at Revolver)
- Origin: Brazil Sul de Minas
- Variety: Icatu
- Process: Anaerobic Natural
- Roast: Espresso
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $95 / 1kg

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Fresh crop from Brazil's peak season.

② [Rarity/Value]
- An anaerobic process gives this classic Brazilian profile an unusual boozy complexity. Great value for a 1kg specialty bag.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Icatu is a classic Brazilian variety, typically forming a nutty and chocolatey base.

**Tastes Like**: Peanut Butter, Grand Marnier, Forest Fruits.

#### Brew Guide (Espresso Machine)
- Dose: 18g
- Ratio: 1:2.5
- Temp: 92°C (lower temp helps balance the booziness for a smoother shot)
- Grind: fine

## How to Choose

For a clean, bright, and elegant pour over, the Luz Helena Pink Bourbon is a clear winner. If you want a wild, jammy, fruit-bomb experience, challenge yourself with the Monogram SL28. On espresso, the DAK pulls a funky, memorable shot best enjoyed on its own. The Ex Animo Superbus is your go-to for milk drinks—it’s consistent, sweet, and forgiving.
