# This Week's Coffee Picks

This week is all about peak season gems and fresh arrivals. We're featuring two distinct pour overs: a classic, elegant Geisha and a modern, experimental Maragogype. For espresso, choose between a super fruity Ethiopian natural or a classic, comforting washed Peruvian blend.

---

## Pour Over Recommendations

### Pick 1: [Costa Rica - Ricardo Hernandez Naranjo - Natural](https://palletcoffeeroasters.com/products/costa-rica-ricardo-hernandez-naranjo-natural)

![Costa Rica - Ricardo Hernandez Naranjo - Natural](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0424/9482/0510/files/300gRicardo-MotiveSeries.png?v=1771538290)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Pallet
- Origin: Costa Rica Tarrazú
- Variety: Geisha
- Process: Natural
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $35

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Peak season for Costa Rican coffee. This lot is fresh and at its best.

② [Rarity/Value]
- A high-quality single-producer Geisha microlot.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Geisha is known for its distinct floral, tea-like, and citrus notes.

**Tastes Like**: passionfruit, blood orange, guava candy.

#### Brew Guide (V60/Pour Over)
- Ratio: 1:16
- Temp: 96°C (Geisha is a high-density bean; higher temps are needed for full extraction of acidity and aromatics.)
- Grind: Medium-coarse

### Pick 2: [DAK Hachi Flow *Enzyflow Natural Process*](https://revolvercoffee.ca/products/dak-hachi-flow-enzyflow-natural-process)

![DAK Hachi Flow *Enzyflow Natural Process*](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0023/8593/6496/files/Screenshot2026-02-09at2.04.05PM.png?v=1770674684)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Revolver (Roasted by DAK)
- Origin: Colombia Cauca
- Variety: Maragogype
- Process: Enzyflow Natural (integrating anaerobic fermentation)
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $43.50/125g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- Fresh arrival from the new harvest, just roasted.

② [Rarity/Value]
- A rare Maragogype variety with a complex process. For those seeking a new experience.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Maragogype beans are very large ("Elephant Beans") and often have a clean, delicate profile.

**Tastes Like**: Grapefruit, Stewed Strawberries, Milky Oolong.

#### Brew Guide (V60/Pour Over)
- Ratio: 1:16
- Temp: 93°C (Heavy fermentation makes this bean more soluble. A slightly lower temp prevents funky, over-extracted flavors.)
- Grind: Medium-coarse

## Espresso Recommendations

### Pick 1: [DAK Jazz Fruits BULK 1KG *Natural Espresso*](https://revolvercoffee.ca/products/dak-jazz-fruits-bulk-1kg-natural-espresso)

![DAK Jazz Fruits BULK 1KG *Natural Espresso*](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0023/8593/6496/files/ScreenShot2024-06-21at10.58.19AM.png?v=1718992724)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Revolver (Roasted by DAK)
- Origin: Ethiopia Daye Bensa
- Variety: Local Landraces
- Process: Natural
- Roast: Medium
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $95/1KG

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- New crop Ethiopian natural, when the fruit notes are most vibrant.

② [Rarity/Value]
- The 1KG bag offers great value for a daily driver espresso.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Classic Ethiopian natural profile—very fruit-forward and expressive.

**Tastes Like**: Strawberry, White Chocolate, Orange.

#### Brew Guide (Espresso Machine)
- Dose: 18g
- Ratio: 1:2.2 (e.g., 18g in to 40g out)
- Temp: 93°C (Balances the fruit acidity and sweetness from the medium roast.)
- Grind: Standard espresso fine

## How to Choose

If you're after a clean, floral, top-tier pour over experience, grab the Pallet Geisha. For a modern, funkier profile, the DAK Maragogype is a wild ride. On espresso, the DAK Ethiopian natural pulls a super juicy SOE shot. The Rogue Wave Peru is your bulletproof choice for lattes—it's classic, chocolatey, and consistent.
