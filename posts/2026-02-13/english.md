# This Week's Coffee Picks

This week's theme is Peak Season vs. Fresh Arrivals. For filter, we have a Colombian Gesha tasting at its absolute peak, alongside a brand new crop Ethiopian natural that just landed. For espresso, two reliable, versatile blends perfect for daily brewing.

---

## Pour Over Recommendations

### Pick 1: [COLOMBIA | Jairo Arcila](https://nemesis.coffee/products/colombia-jairo-arcila)

![COLOMBIA | Jairo Arcila](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0570/9499/6149/files/Pink_Front.png?v=1748633034)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Nemesis
- Origin: Colombia Quindio
- Variety: Gesha
- Process: Washed
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $39 / 250g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- It's peak season for Colombian coffees, meaning the flavor is vibrant and at its most expressive.

② [Rarity/Value]
- A micro-lot from esteemed producer Jairo Arcila. This is a benchmark example of a classic, clean washed Gesha.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: The Gesha variety is known for its intense floral, aromatic, and tea-like qualities.

**Tastes Like**: Rosewater, mandarin orange, white peach juice.

#### Brew Guide (V60/Pour Over)
- Ratio: 1:16
- Temp: 96°C (High-altitude, dense beans like this Gesha need higher thermal energy for full extraction.)
- Grind: Medium-fine

### Pick 2: [Ethiopia - Halo Beriti | Special Prep Natural](https://roguewavecoffee.ca/products/ethiopia-halo-beriti-special-prep-natural)

![Ethiopia - Halo Beriti | Special Prep Natural](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0042/0773/8992/files/EthiopiaHaloBeritiNatural2026340gBAG.jpg?v=1770491195)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Rogue Wave
- Origin: Ethiopia Halo Beriti
- Variety: Heirloom
- Process: Natural Special Prep
- Roast: Light
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $27 / 250g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- A fresh arrival. This is one of the first new-crop Ethiopians of the season.

② [Rarity/Value]
- "Special Prep" indicates meticulous, multi-stage hand sorting, resulting in a very low defect count and a clean cup.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: Ethiopian naturals are famous for their bold, dominant fruit-forward profiles.

**Tastes Like**: Blueberry jam, strawberry candy, red grape. Note: Recommend resting for 3+ weeks for flavors to settle.

#### Brew Guide (V60/Pour Over)
- Ratio: 1:16
- Temp: 90°C (Roaster's recommendation, likely to control the heavy fermentation notes and prevent astringency.)
- Grind: Medium

## Espresso Recommendations

### Pick 1: [Ex Animo Superbus BULK 1KG  *Espresso*](https://revolvercoffee.ca/products/ex-animo-superbus-bulk-1kg-espresso)

![Ex Animo Superbus BULK 1KG  *Espresso*](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0023/8593/6496/files/Screenshot2025-03-26at1.03.22PM.png?v=1743455815)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Revolver (featuring Ex Animo)
- Origin: Central America & Africa Blend
- Variety: Blend
- Process: Blend
- Roast: Medium
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $59 / 1kg

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- The Central American component is at peak season, providing a solid, sweet foundation.

② [Rarity/Value]
- Excellent value in the 1kg format, making it a great workhorse espresso for high-volume home use.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: A blend engineered for balance and versatility, with a focus on sweetness.

**Tastes Like**: Brown sugar, milk chocolate, crisp apple.

#### Brew Guide (Espresso Machine)
- Dose: 18g
- Ratio: 1:2 - 1:2.1 (for a 36-38g yield)
- Temp: 93°C (A standard temperature for balancing sweetness and acidity.)
- Grind: Fine

### Pick 2: [Daily Driver - Seasonal Blend: Brazil + Ethiopia](https://palletcoffeeroasters.com/products/daily-driver)

![Daily Driver - Seasonal Blend: Brazil + Ethiopia](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0424/9482/0510/files/300gEarlyRiser_e1776922-d231-4939-a708-a9b273c3a6b7.png?v=1770929451)


#### Coffee Profile
- Roaster: Pallet
- Origin: Brazil & Ethiopia
- Variety: Blend
- Process: Natural
- Roast: Medium
- Roast Date: Recent
- Price: $20 / 250g

#### Why Get This
① [Seasonality/Status]
- The dominant Brazil component (75%) is in its peak season window, ensuring a fresh and stable base.

② [Rarity/Value]
- A classic, comforting flavor profile that is extremely reliable for milk drinks.

#### Flavor Profile
**Variety Character**: A modern take on a traditional chocolate-forward blend, with added fruit and floral complexity.

**Tastes Like**: Dark chocolate, raspberry, a hint of jasmine.

#### Brew Guide (Espresso Machine)
- Dose: 19g
- Ratio: 1:2 (for a 38g yield)
- Temp: 92°C (A slightly lower temp helps avoid over-extracting bitter compounds from the more developed roast.)
- Grind: Fine

## How to Choose

For a clean, elegant pour over, the Nemesis Gesha is a standout—perfect for a special occasion brew. If you're chasing the intense fruit bomb of a fresh Ethiopian natural, grab the Rogue Wave. For espresso, Revolver's Superbus is the versatile, cost-effective choice for any drink style. The Pallet Daily Driver is your go-to for rich, chocolatey lattes and flat whites.
